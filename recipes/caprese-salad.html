Caprese Salad Recipe

Description

Caprese salad is a simple and refreshing Italian salad made with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, basil leaves, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze. It is a perfect appetizer or side dish for a summer meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe tomatoes
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Slice the tomatoes and mozzarella cheese into thick slices.
  2. Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices on a serving plate, alternating them.
  3. Place a fresh basil leaf on top of each tomato and mozzarella slice.
  4. Drizzle the salad with extra-virgin olive oil.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Finish the salad by drizzling balsamic glaze over the top.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
